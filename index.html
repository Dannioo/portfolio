Welcome to my portfolio site! I hope that all my visitors can not only get a sense of the techniques behind my work, but also what I aim to accomplish with every project. My personal philosophy on interactive experiences is that they are the most powerful storytelling medium of today's society, and as a designer, I have a duty to use all the facets that only this medium provides to tell stories that are representative of not only different cultures, but also the different values and philosophies that are behind them. This philosophy has carried over to my roles as a SEO specialist, and I strive to put forward the best marketing portfolio for each unique client I work with. I'm lucky that each company I work with has dared to do things differently, just like how the best digital experiences aren't afraid to be scrutinized by the entire world.

ABOUT

Born in Alabama and raised in the northern suburbs of Atlanta, I fell in love with creative arts from a young age. While I also spent time attending art and music classes both in school and after school, I was also very curious about everything in the world around me, and the same sense of adventure that made me fall in love with adventure games from a young age translated into my desire to join the Boy Scouts. I would eventually earn his Eagle Scout during my senior year of high school. I wanted a way to combine his love for playing games, his interest in the internet, and his skill for creating art and enrolled in the Integrated Digital Media program at New York University to learn not only game development but to further evolve my stance on art and how it can be integrated into the technology around us.

I chose to come to NYU because I thought it would expand my horizons and meet more people than I would be able to if I had stayed in the South. During his first year, I joined the Violet Leadership Institute at NYU to develop my leadership style, which is based on inclusion and support of all members. I would use then this style to site lead an Alternative Breaks trip in public health to my home city of Atlanta, where I saw the intersectionality between the healthcare system and my own perspective, and to learn the difficulties that his fellow students face in their own studies and experience in New York.

Despite branching out into all sorts of areas during my time at college, my passion for making games and websites and having fun with everyone of all walks of life has never once faded. Everything I have done was with the intention of learning of how I can make experiences enjoyable for all people, and my studies at NYU have all been geared towards development and design. I have since used this skillset I've developed as a creative technologist and applied it to a successful career in technical SEO and digital marketing.
